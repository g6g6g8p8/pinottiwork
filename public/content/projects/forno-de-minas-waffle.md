## The Brief

Forno de Minas is the brand that made the Brazilian pão de queijo a household staple. Their waffle — the second-biggest product in the portfolio — was still largely unknown to Brazilian shoppers, even though it's a daily staple in the US and Europe. The brief: open up the category and introduce the product to new households.

## The Idea

**"Combina com tudo" — Goes With Everything.** A digital-first campaign built around the waffle's versatility, with both the traditional and the light-whole-grain versions playing the role of a blank canvas for anything you put on top.

The platform extended into in-store merchandising, tabloids and sampling at schools and events across Belo Horizonte, São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro and Curitiba — plus a recipe contest with food bloggers built around the product.
