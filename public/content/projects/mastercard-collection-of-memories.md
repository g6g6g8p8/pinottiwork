## The Idea

Some things you buy. Some things you keep. **Coleção de Memórias** — Collection of Memories — is a pair of short films exploring the difference: the everyday gestures, the people, the trips that quietly add up to who we are.

The films sit in the same emotional territory as Mastercard's global "Priceless" platform, translated into a distinctly Brazilian register.
