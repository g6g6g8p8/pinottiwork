## The Campaign

In Brazil, the credit card tends to be cast as the villain of the household budget. Supereconomics flipped that frame: a financial education content series showing that you don't need superpowers to save money, organize bills and use credit responsibly — you just need the right habits.

The campaign rolled out as a cast of everyday superheroes, each tied to one money skill: paying the bills on time, mastering the card, breaking down expenses, stretching what you earn.
