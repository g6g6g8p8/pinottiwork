## The Brief

Mitsubishi Motors sponsored more than 200 tennis matches on ESPN throughout 2021 — Australian Open, US Open, Masters 1000, ATP 500 and the ATP Finals/Nitto among them. Going beyond standard media placement, the brand wanted to show how its 4x4 DNA actually connects with the sport.

## The Idea

Off-road, Mitsubishi owns the idea of rally. On a tennis court, a rally is an intense exchange of shots back and forth. **4YOU4RALLY** lived in the overlap between those two worlds.

## The Films

Three full-3D short films follow a Mitsubishi Pajero Sport crossing forest, plantation and beach until it arrives at tennis courts built in the middle of nowhere — collapsing the two universes into one image.
