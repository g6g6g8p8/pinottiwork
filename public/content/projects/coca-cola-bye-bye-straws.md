## The Challenge

Coca-Cola is one of the brands that did the most to make plastic straws a part of everyday life. Now that we know what plastic waste does to the planet, the brand had a responsibility to act.

## Insight

If the brand had once put straws into culture, it could now take them out — starting with the very images that built the habit.

[video](https://vimeo.com/331835891 "Bye Bye, Straws")

## The Campaign

It started with a simple PR action: the most-liked Instagram photo of all time at the time was an ad of Selena Gomez drinking a Coke with a straw. We recreated the exact same post — without the straw. From there, the campaign reopened the Coca-Cola archive and redrew classic ads, this time straw-free.
