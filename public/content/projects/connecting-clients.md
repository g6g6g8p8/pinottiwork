For Next Ip, adapting is as essential as breathing. With over 20 years of experience in the communications industry, operating across Brazil, resilience is not just a trend - it is the backbone of the company.

[video](https://i.imgur.com/pGw735P.mp4 "Concept")

When the marketing team needed to evolve, Next Ip invited Agência Crush to lead a complete rebranding. The result? A new visual identity inspired by the company's core purpose: connections. This vision also shaped the creation of a fresh tagline, developed through in-depth research with internal and external audiences.
