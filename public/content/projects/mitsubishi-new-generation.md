## The Brief

Mitsubishi Motors wanted to reposition the brand in Brazil around a new generation — people building a "4x4 lifestyle" on their own terms. The launch piece for the platform also doubled as the launch film for the new L200 Triton Sport 2021.

## The Idea

A new world calls for new symbols. The campaign portrays a generation rewriting what success, family and leadership look like: agribusiness entrepreneurs using technology to drive productivity, more women in leadership, and a more diverse, plural idea of family. The brand's iconic 4x4 spirit stays — only now seen through the lens of who's actually driving it today.

From this campaign onward, every Mitsubishi product carries the new "**4you4**" signature.
