## The Brief

Launch the first edition of **Mastercard Jazz**, a free jazz festival held at Auditório Ibirapuera in São Paulo, with a line-up that put new Brazilian voices alongside international names — Christian Scott aTunde Adjuah, Bixiga 70, Lourenço Rebetez & Xênia França.

## The Idea

Photography as the headline act. Instead of generic concert imagery, the campaign was built around portraits by **Maurício Nahas** — frames with movement, modernity and grain that speak to both purist jazz fans and a new generation discovering the genre through digital platforms.

Out-of-home took over bus shelters and prime urban surfaces; on Instagram Stories, the festival broadcast live snippets and chats with the artists across the two days.
