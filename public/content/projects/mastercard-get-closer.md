Mastercard was the official Conmebol Copa América 2019 sponsor and the official sponsor of the Brazilian national squad. The brief was unambiguous: make the sponsorship stand for something only Mastercard could own. No logo placement, no generic football energy. A real idea.

The tension was real. Contactless payment technology was already widespread in Brazil — every POS terminal in the country carried the word *aproxime* ("bring closer") as a functional instruction. But the behaviour hadn't yet become habit. Brazilians knew the word. They just hadn't made it theirs.

That gap was the opportunity.

---

## The Idea: Aproxime / Get Closer

*Aproxime* is printed on every contactless POS terminal in Brazil. It's a technical instruction — tap your card, bring it closer. But in Portuguese, it carries a second register entirely. *Aproximar* means to draw near, to close the distance between people. And that's exactly how Brazilians live football: together, warm, physically close.

The dual meaning became the campaign's spine. **Get Closer. Start Something Priceless.**

Functional technology meets emotional truth — both living in a single word that Mastercard already owned on every payment surface in the country.

## Activations

Beyond the brand film, *Get Closer* drove a series of activations across the tournament:

The **Impossible Mascots** — children with special needs walked onto the field alongside the Brazilian national team, turning the sponsorship into a human moment that transcended football.

[video](https://player.vimeo.com/video/817092515?h=0845daaeae "Impossible Mascots")

**Murilo** — the story of a Brazilian boy and passionate Argentine fan, a portrait of the kind of cross-border connection that only football makes possible.

[video](https://player.vimeo.com/video/876537385?h=2792316215 "Murilo — The Brazilian boy with an Argentine passion")

A **crowd activation** that united thousands of voices in a single chant across stadiums.

[video](https://player.vimeo.com/video/836365351?h=50a20ab83d "Crowd's United Chant")

## Outcome

Card-usage expectations were exceeded. The campaign became a global reference inside the Mastercard network. On the main brand tracker index, *Get Closer* surpassed VISA for the first time — a new record that had never been achieved on the account.
