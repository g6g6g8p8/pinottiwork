## The Campaign

An acceptance campaign for Mastercard built around a simple promise: with the card in your hand, paying feels like magic. The pieces took everyday Brazilian scenes — the beach kiosk, the street market, the movie theater — and reimagined the act of paying as a sleight of hand.
