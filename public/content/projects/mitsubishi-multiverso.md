## The Brief

Launch the Outlander Sport 2021 and introduce its positioning: **"the new diamond of Mitsubishi"** — a nod to the brand's three-diamond DNA and its 4x4 heritage.

## The Idea

Diamonds only appear under extreme pressure, and only truly shine when they catch the light. The film translates that into a journey: the Outlander Sport crossing a string of possible universes, holding its shape through every kind of pressure.

An original track was written for the campaign by Flavio Waiteman (Tech and Soul) and Otávio de Moraes (BloodAudio), and recorded in New York with singer **Tansu**.
