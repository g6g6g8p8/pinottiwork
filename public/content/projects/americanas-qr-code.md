## The Campaign

We introduced QR codes into Brazilian television merchandising — a format that had always been built around a host reading a script and a logo on screen. With a single scannable code, the audience could go from the couch to the Americanas app in seconds. The results spoke for themselves: after this, every TV merchandising slot started carrying a QR code.
